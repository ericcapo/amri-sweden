Aquatic Microbiome Research Initiative (AMRI)

The aim of the Aquatic Microbiome Research Initiative is to engage leading Swedish researchers with backgrounds in microbial ecology, evolution and geochemistry, in a collaborative effort to advance our understanding of aquatic microorganisms and their functional role in the environment.

Our current objectives are:

  • Communicate via a mailing list. Each subscriber will be able to share information about job positions, conferences, and newspapers via our mailing list.
  • Organise an annual meeting, the first one in Uppsala in 2025 (dates to be determined)
